About Me

I am an HVAC Site Engineer with hands-on experience in execution, installation, testing, and commissioning of HVAC systems for data centers, airports, and industrial projects. I have worked on large-scale projects including 20+ MW data center facilities, ensuring reliable and efficient cooling solutions.

My professional journey includes working with air-cooled and water-cooled chillers, PAC, PAHU, FWU, VRF/VRV systems, cooling towers, PHE, CDU, and chilled water piping. I am actively involved in site execution, contractor coordination, inspections, and commissioning activities in both fit-out and operational environments.

I strongly believe in quality workmanship, safety compliance, and adherence to engineering standards. I enjoy working on mission-critical HVAC infrastructure where precision, performance, and reliability are essential.
